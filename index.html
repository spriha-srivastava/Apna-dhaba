Gulam Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Mawa
  • Oil
  • Maida

Recipe

  1. Mix 1 cup khoya, 2 tbsp flour, and a pinch of baking soda to form a smooth dough, then shape into small balls.
  2. Fry the balls on low heat until golden brown.
  3. Prepare a sugar syrup by boiling 1 cup sugar with 1 1/2 cups water and cardamom.
  4. Soak the fried gulab jamuns in the warm syrup for at least 1 hour before serving.

KajuKatli

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • kaju
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Grind 1 cup of cashews into a fine powder.
  2. In a pan, heat 1/2 cup sugar and 1/4 cup water to form a syrup (1 string consistency).
  3. Add the cashew powder to the syrup, stirring continuously to form a dough.
  4. Knead the dough once cooled slightly, then roll it out and cut into diamond shapes.
  5. Garnish with silver vark and let it set before serving.

Laddoo

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Besan
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Roast 2 cups of besan (gram flour) in ghee until golden brown and aromatic.
  2. Add 1 cup powdered sugar, 1/4 tsp cardamom powder, and mix well.
  3. Let it cool slightly, then shape into small laddoos.
  4. Garnish with chopped nuts and enjoy!

Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Lemaon juice
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Boil 1 liter milk, curdle it with lemon juice to make chhena (paneer), and strain the whey.
  2. Knead the chhena into a smooth dough, shape into small balls.
  3. In a pot, boil 1 cup sugar with 4 cups water to make syrup, then add the chhena balls.
  4. Cook for 15-20 minutes, until rasgullas double in size, and serve chilled.

Jallebi

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Maida
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Make a batter by mixing 1 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tbsp cornflour, 1/4 tsp turmeric, and water; let it ferment for 6-8 hours.
  2. Heat sugar with water to make a sticky syrup (1 string consistency) and add saffron or cardamom.
  3. Pipe the batter into hot oil in spiral shapes, fry until golden and crisp.
  4. Dip the fried jalebis in warm sugar syrup for a few seconds, then serve hot.
